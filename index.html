--- layout: home author_profile: true --- Title: Bioinformatic Analysis of Influenza Hemagglutinin Surface Protein Across the 20th Century\ Authors: Priyanka Bhatnagar and Nikkia Zarabian\ Data: We will be analyzing the nucleotide and amino acid sequences of surface protein hemagglutinin for major epidemics of influenza in the past century; this data will be obtained utilizing GenBank. This project aims to study critical mutations in the active sites of hemagglutinin to understand how influenza has evolved in the past century
